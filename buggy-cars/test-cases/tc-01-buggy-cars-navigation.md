# Test Case: Buggy Cars Navigation

**Test Case ID:** TC-001  
**Objective:** Verify that clicking on car models from any section navigates to the correct car model page.  
**Preconditions:** User has access to https://buggy.justtestit.org/

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the homepage.  
2. Click on any car brand in the Popular Make section.  
3. Click on any car model image.  

## Expected Result
The page navigates to the correct car model details page.  

## Actual Result
Page loads indefinitely.  

## Severity
Major
