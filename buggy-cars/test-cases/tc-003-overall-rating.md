# Test Case: Navigation via Overall Rating Section

**Test Case ID:** TC-003  
**Objective:** Verify that clicking on Overall Rating section navigates to the correct section.  
**Preconditions:** User has access to https://buggy.justtestit.org/  

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the homepage.  
2. Click on Overall Rating section.   

## Expected Result
The page navigates to the correct Overall Rating section list page.    

## Actual Result
Navigation works correctly - The correct Buggy Cars Rating section loads.
