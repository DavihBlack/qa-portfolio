# Test Case: Add Product to Cart

**Test Case ID:** TC-003  
**Objective:** Verify that users can successfully add products to the cart from the PLP and PDP.  
**Preconditions:** User is logged in to https://www.saucedemo.com  

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the Product Listing Page (PLP).  
2. Select any available product.  
3. Click the "Add to Cart" button on the PLP.  
4. Navigate to the Product Detail Page (PDP) for the same product.  
5. Click the "Add to Cart" button on the PDP.  
6. Open the cart and verify the product is listed correctly.  

## Expected Result
- Product is successfully added to the cart from both PLP and PDP.  
- Cart displays correct product name, image, and price.
