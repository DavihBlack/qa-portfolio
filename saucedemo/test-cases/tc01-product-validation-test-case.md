# Test Case: Sauce Labs Product

**Test Case ID:** TC-001  
**Objective:** Verify that product images and prices are consistent between PLP and PDP.  
**Preconditions:** User is logged in to https://www.saucedemo.com  

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the Product Listing Page (PLP).  
2. Locate a product.  
3. Check the product image and price.  
4. Click on the product to go to the Product Detail Page (PDP).  
5. Verify the image and price on the PDP.  

## Expected Result
- Product image and price on PLP match the PDP.  

## Actual Result
- PLP shows incorrect image for the "Sauce Labs Backpack".  
- Price on PLP does not match PDP.  

## Severity
Medium
