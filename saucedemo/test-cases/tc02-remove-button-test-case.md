# Test Case: Remove Product from Cart

**Test Case ID:** TC-002  
**Objective:** Verify that products can be removed from the cart on PLP and PDP.  
**Preconditions:** User is logged in to https://www.saucedemo.com and has at least one product in the cart.  

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to PLP.  
2. Add a product to the cart.  
3. Click the "Remove" button on PLP.  
4. Navigate to PDP for the same product.  
5. Click the "Remove" button on PDP.  

## Expected Result
The product is removed from the cart on both PLP and PDP.  

## Actual Result
Remove button does not remove product from the cart.  

## Severity
High
